Bill Gates

Here's a timeline about Bill Gate's life:

  • 1955 - Bill Gates was born on October 28, 1955.
  • 1975 - Gates dropped out of harvard and co-founded Microsoft Corp. with Paul Allen.
  • 1985 - Gates introduced the Microsoft Windows operating system (OS).
  • 1987 - He became the youngest US billionaire, reportedly worth $1.25 billion at just 31 years old.
  • 2005 - Gates was awarded an honorary knighthood by Queen Elizabeth for outstanding contribution to enterprise.
  • 2006 - He announced he would give up on his daily role at Microsoft to concentrate on humanitarian and educational interests.
  • 2010 - The Gates foundation announced it will donate $10 billion for vaccine research over the next 10 years.
  • 2019 - As of 2019, the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation has issued $54.8 billion in grant payments to charitable causes.
  • 2020 - In March, it's announced that gates is leaving the boards of Microsoft and Berkshire Hathaway to "dedicate more time to his philanthropic priorities".
